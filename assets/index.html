What's a Home Assistant?
Home Assistants are innovative devices which hopee to redefine the way we live our lives.

Beginning with Amazon's "Echo" in 2014, tech companies worldwide have been innovating in an effort to bring Artifial Intelligence and Hardware together to create the perfect bond with consumers.

Home Assistants are useful for many things like getting the weather, setting timers, looking up facts on the internet, and even things such as turn off lights in a home.
